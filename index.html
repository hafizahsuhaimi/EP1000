Welcome to My Website!

Hello! Welcome to my website! This is where I will document my work and each of my assignments at. This website was created for my elective module, EP1000, Digital Fabrication Prototyping Fundamentals. This elective teaches students to learn how to use digital fabrication techniques in designing and creating prototypes. Students will also learn to document and publish their work. This module uses computer aided design techniques, 3D Printing and laser cutting methods to develop students' prototypes.

I hope you find this website helpful and informative. Do check out my different assignments I have done to get a better understanding of this module!
